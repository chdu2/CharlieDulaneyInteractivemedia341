My Grandma Told Stories or Cautionary Tales

By: Marlanda Dekine-Sapient Soul


 

                    Grandma Thelma told me                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Grandma Thelma told me                                                                                                                                                                                                  Grandma Thelma told me

               if I slept on my back                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           I might see a plat eye                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 I’d feel welcomed by the eye.

                                                            a hag would ride me and I’d never return, so I trained                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          when I was seven years old. I could imagine                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 I’d be made to feel safe and loved,






                                                                                                                                     my body to sleep on my stomach. Even now,                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        cypress knees growing full bodies                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        but if I looked directly into that big white bodied cornea,








                                                                                                                                                                                                                         if I wake up cold, I open my mouth                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   to walk the hungry road, an entire body                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              I’d forget where I came from.










                                                                                                                                                                         and repeat the 23rd Psalm.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   of eye nicknamed Plat.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    I’d be disappeared.
